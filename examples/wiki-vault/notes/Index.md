## Entries

- [[Topic]] — exact basename, only one file, resolves.
- [[Subtopic]] — two files share this basename; shortest-path wins
  and resolves to the root-level `notes/Subtopic.md`.
- [[archive/Subtopic]] — disambiguates to the archived copy via the
  parent-suffix rule.
- [[Topic#Conventions]] — anchor matches Topic's raw heading text.
- [[Topic|See the topic page]] — alias does not affect resolution.
